Project Principles

Learn Publicly

You only really understand a thing when you can explain it. You get another resource for learning, and I get a better understanding.

Share the mess

We shy away from showing off our mistakes and subpar projects. We all have them. There is value in the mess, not just in what comes of it.

Record notes

A lead devloper I worked with once told me, "I figure any time I write an email to more than 2 people, it should be in a wiki so anyone can refer to it."
